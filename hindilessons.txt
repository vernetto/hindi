What is your name? tumhaara (your) naam kya (what) hai ? तुम्हारा नाम क्या हे?

तुम्हारा   tumhaara = त  ta  ु  u  म    ma ्   ह  ha  ा  aa  र   ra ा   aa

ु  u  is the combining form (matra) of उ  u
्  virama to suppress the default vowel after a consonant
ा   diacritic for aa

नाम  naam = न  na  ा   aa म   ma

क्या kya = क  ka  ्   य  ya  ा aa
